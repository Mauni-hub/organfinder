Understanding Organ Donation in India

Learn about the critical importance of organ donation and how it saves lives across our nation

The Current Situation

India faces a severe organ shortage with over 500,000 people waiting for organ transplants. Only about 0.34 per million population donate organs, compared to 36.88 in Spain. This gap costs thousands of lives annually.

Organ Viability Timeline

How Donation Saves Lives

A single organ donor can save up to 8 lives and enhance the lives of 50+ others through tissue donation. Organs like kidneys, liver, heart, lungs, pancreas, and corneas can give recipients a second chance at life.

The Role of Technology

Real-time organ availability systems like OrganFinder help bridge the gap between donors and recipients, ensuring that viable organs reach patients within the critical time window for successful transplantation.
